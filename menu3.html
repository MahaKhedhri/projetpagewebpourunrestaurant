Menu

The restaurant Through the ages is a real institution in Ascona . It is located directly on the lake promenade. In the warm season, the facades all in windows open onto the terrace at the Lungolago, in autumn and winter, guests enjoy an exceptional view from the vaulted room on the lake. The menu offers a tempting combination of local and international specialties , masterfully prepared from the best products. The delicacies are accompanied by noble wines from our cellar which houses a collection of exquisite Italian and French wines and local wines from the region.
